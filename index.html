Streamian

All-In-One Simple & Automated Streaming Platform | M7/Movian Media Center | Playstation 3

Features

  • Supports TPB, EZTV, YTS and Archive.org. Plus you can create your own addons then add them in the settings page. I will create a guide for this soon.
  • Save Your Favorite Shows, Movies & Channels to Your Library!
  • Filter Out H.265 Videos! (For Playstation 3) / older Android Devices
  • Automatically Picks the Best Source Based On Your Preferred Video Quality & Seeder Count!
  • Use Together with Official Movian/M7 Subtitles Addons!
  • Browse a range of Free-To-Air TV Channel Providers like Pluto TV, Redbox and more!

How to Install (Official Plugin Repository)

  1. Navigate to Plugins > Browse Available Plugins > Video Streaming > Streamian
  2. Select Install.
  3. Return to Movian/M7 Home and select Streamian.
  4. From Streamian, open the side menu and select "Update Streamian".

Note: Open the side menu from any page inside Streamian and select "Update Streamian" periodically to recieve updates.
